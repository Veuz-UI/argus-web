Argus logistics

Warehousing Facility

We provide spacious warehousing facilities in prime locations at affordable prices. Government Department approvals and Insurance is taken care of by Argus. Air-conditioned Warehouses available for the taking. Licensed forklift operators are deployed at Warehouses by Argus.

We also have Open Yard facilities for use complete with CCTV monitoring and asphalted ground best suited to business requirements.

Argus has a powerful distribution fleet ready at your service consisting of 3 Ton Mitsubishi pickups, available for rent at reasonable prices.

Argus also has food warehousing and freight services. We ensure temperature controlled, scrutinized storage of bulk food items as well as careful, spoilage-free transportation of food.
