Company Profile

Being a company established in 2011 ARGUS MIDDLE EAST W.L.L. is a trading company operating from Doha with all infrastructures and back up to excel in the market. We meant for the corporate control and the absolute engagement of our strength as well as to utilize maximum business opportunities of international market.

We supply Construction materials, Construction Chemicals and Ironmongeries, industrial equipments and tools to its clientele in Qatar. The company imports general building materials, sanitary fittings, and Kitchen Cabinets as well as all kinds of hardware items.

A group of well equipped marketing professionals and skilled installation team enhances our reputation level.

Our marketing networks and reliable contacts with potential clients always help us to bring in all the project references have been well appreciated and respected by our customers and architectures.
